DEFINITIONS

Personal Information: Personal Information means data about a living individual who can be identified from those data (or from those and other information either in our possession or likely to come into our possession).

Voluntary Information: Voluntary Information means data provided willingly by an individual which may not direct identify them (for example, voluntarily posting content or providing public feedback to community content).

Usage Data: Usage Data is data collected automatically either generated by the use of the Service or from the Service infrastructure itself (for example, the duration of a page visit).

Cookies: Cookies are small, unique pieces of data stored on a User's device.

Third-Party: Third-Party represents any additional legal business or company which may access your data upon “your” agreement with “us”, the Application.

Data Controller: Data Controller means a natural or legal person who (either alone or jointly or in common with other persons) determines the purposes for which and the manner in which any personal data are, or are to be, processed. Third-Party (or Service Providers): Data Processor (or Service Provider) means any natural or legal person who processes the data on behalf of the Data Controller. We may use the services of various Service Providers in order to process your data more effectively.

Data Subject: Data Subject is any living individual who is the subject of Personal Information and Voluntary Information.

User: The User is the individual using the Application. The User corresponds to the Data Subject, who is the subject of Personal Data.
