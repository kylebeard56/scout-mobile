Frequently Asked Questions

Everyone has curiousities - we hope this section will pique yours and give you the answers you need.
If your question isn't answered, we encourage you to reach out to us below.

  • How is this different from Google or Yelp?

    Great question! Google and Yelp aim to give you a surface-level view of what businesses are around you, but they don't dive deep into what a business offers beyond their standard menu and a pile of reviews which you can never fully trust. Sure, you can get the store hours easily and see if the place serves chicken tenders for your kids, but what about their fun events? What makes them a dive bar? What are their happy hour? Is this a place I can go with my friends and family and actually have fun? And if the answer is yes to any of these questions, then why? Well, this is where Scout thrives and we want to showcase the smallest of details that truly make a business special. Naturally, we find that small and local businesses are the ones that have the most to offer in terms of little details, and therefore we help them advertise and market what makes them unique and a treasure to the community.

  • Why should a business put extra time into keeping up with this app?

    We live in an instant gratification world. You log onto Facebook or Instagram and scroll to get your 'fix'. As a business, you try to harness this physcological need by advertising and posting as much as possible on these social media platforms. Yet, there's a disconnect still. You submit a post and hope users see it to get your message across. You rely on your audience being in the right place at the right time to get exposure. Scout works in the opposite sense. We act as the hub of information just as much as the tool to deliver it. Posting on our application gets you the exposure that makes your time worthy because your audience no longer needs to go out of the way to find you. You are always ready to be discovered on the map and you are always on full display at the right time. Therefore, you can come to them... from the comfort of your business door step.

  • Why is the map scouting area so small?

    We tweaked our algorithm to provide you with the fastest loading speeds. When the map is scouted, it gives you a 10km by 10km (~6 mile diameter) search area of what is happening nearby! We felt this was a nice balance between speed and geographic coverage. If you're looking for a specific area, just drag or zoom on the map and re-search the area!

  • As a business owner, how do I apply to own my content?

    There are two ways:

    1. When submitting a new post (tapping the feather icon), when you enter a venue name, you'll see a button at the bottom that says 'Own this business?'. Tap it.

    2. When visiting the venue, you'll see a button that says 'Own this business?' if the business isn't already verified (i.e. private). Tap it.

    Tapping will prompt you to apply as a venue representative. Once you apply, we will review your application. If approved, you'll be able to manage the private venue in the Settings. If denied, we will give good reason.

  • Why is this only available on iOS?

    Resources are limited! We want to develop another mobile app for Android and also make a website, but we're short on time and cash to get the job done. Since Scout is currently free to all users (beyond donations), we don't make a profit! However, if you'd really like to see an Android or website created, feel free to reach out and help us contribute however you can!

  • When will posting be available outside of South Carolina?

    We're rolling the app out in waves, so once we work out the bugs and kinks, we'll quickly open it up to the southeastern states, then to the entire United States, then North America, and finally worldwide! We appreciate your patience during our trial period.
