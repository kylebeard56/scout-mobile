We are a local food + drink forum that allows users within a community to rally behind their small businesses to share and promote their live deals and events.

About Us

Scout is a revolutionary iOS application that aims to be a single source for businesses and their fans to easily view and post great deals and events happening nearby in their local community. You'll be able to discover exciting opportunities and never waste time brainstorming your future plans again.

On the Fly

Scout helps you quickly get the scoop on events happening live and locally. You’ll never waste time searching for fun again.

Plan Ahead

Scout lets you look into the future to easily make plans with family, friends, and colleagues.

  • Looking for dog-friendly venues, draft beer, or TVs? Scout lets you plan your destination spot by narrowing down live and upcoming options you'll love.
  • Planning your after work escape? Fire up Scout, rally the squad, clock out, and hit your next happy hour.
  • Going out of town? Set your location to a new city to plan ahead.
  • In a time crunch? Instantly sort posts by live, popular, or closest to you to save time on the fly.
  • Need to inform others? Quickly share events with Scout through iMessage, email, or other services.
  • Have the insider scoop? Post content about deals or events for others to enjoy in a matter of seconds.
  • See a mistake? Well we trust you, and we let our users edit and moderate content as long as it meets our Community Content Standards.

Please note that Scout is currently in a pilot trial and only allows posting in the state of South Carolina. Don't fret - expansion to new states is coming soon! While we do not have an Android or Web-based application, we hope to gain success to develop those in the future as well.
