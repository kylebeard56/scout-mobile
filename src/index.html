About Us

Scout wants you to have fun and make memories, all while saving some money in the process. Our goal is to partner and collaborate with local businesses to share and promote the deals and events that gives them their unique character.

On the Fly

Scout helps you quickly get the scoop on deals and events happening live and locally. You’ll be able to discover exciting opportunities and never waste time searching for fun again.

Plan Ahead

Scout's intuitive interface allows you to instantly see when deals or events will occur in the future to easily make plans with family, friends, and colleagues.

  • Looking for dog-friendly venues with draft beer and TVs? Scout lets you plan your destination spot by filtering live and upcoming options you'll love.
  • Planning your after work escape? Fire up Scout, rally the squad, clock out, and hit your next happy hour.
  • Stumped on date night? Grab your significant other and scout your evening in the app!
  • In a time crunch? Instantly sort posts by live, popular, or closest to you to save time on the fly.
  • Going out of town? Set your location to a new city to plan ahead.
  • Need to inform others? Quickly share events with Scout through iMessage, email, or other services.
  • Have the insider scoop? Post content about deals or events for others to enjoy in a matter of seconds.
  • See a mistake? We trust you to edit and moderate public posts that meet our Community Content Standards.

  • How does this help businesses?

    If you're a business, you want customers in the door. Good news - we want that too! We want market for you to deliver information to your fans and connect you with potential customers on a level deeper than other apps. It's a win-win.

    There's customers using Scout looking for "you". Yet, they can't find you and worse, they might not even know you exists, which we feel is a shame. Use Scout to bridge this discovery today.

  • Don't Yelp and Foursquare do this?

    Great question! Google, Yelp, and Foursquare aim to give you a surface-level view of what businesses are around you, but they don't dive deep into what a business offers beyond their standard menu and a pile of reviews which you can never fully trust.

    Sure, you can get the store hours easily and see if the place serves chicken tenders for your kids, but what about their fun events? What makes them a dive bar? What are their happy hours? Is this a place I can go with my friends and family and actually have fun? This is where Scout thrives.

    Naturally, we find that small and local businesses are the ones that have the biggest story to tell in terms of makes them unique and a treasure to the community. Use Scout to tell this story.

  • How does this differ from other marketing?

    We live in an instant gratification world. You log onto Facebook or Instagram and scroll to get your 'fix'. As a business, you try to harness this physcological need by advertising and posting as much as possible on these social media platforms. You rely on your audience being in the right place at the right time to get exposure, but let's face the facts - they're likely scrolling through vacation photos and selfie stories. Scout works in the opposite sense.

    We act as the hub of information just as much as the tool to deliver it. Posting on our application gets you the exposure that makes your time worthy because your audience no longer needs to go out of the way to find you. You are always ready to be discovered on the map and you are always on full display at the appropriate time. There's no chance to it.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everyone has curiousities - we hope this section will pique yours and give you the answers you need.
If your question isn't answered, we encourage you to reach out to us below.

  • Why is the map scouting area so small?

    We tweaked our algorithm to provide you with the fastest loading speeds. When the map is scouted, it gives you a 10km by 10km (~6 mile diameter) search area of what is happening nearby! We felt this was a nice balance between speed and geographic coverage. If you're looking for a specific area, just drag or zoom on the map and re-scout the area!

    If you want an even more concentrated search, tap the binocular icon and discover live events within the blue box.

  • As a business owner, how do I apply to own my content?

    There are two ways:

    1. When submitting a new post (tapping the feather icon), when you enter a venue name, you'll see a button at the bottom that says 'Own this business?'. Tap it.

    2. When visiting your venue's page, you'll see a button that says 'Own this business?' if the business isn't already verified (i.e. private). Tap it.

    Tapping either will prompt you to apply as a venue representative. Once you apply, we will review your application. If approved, you'll be able to manage the private venue in the Settings. If denied, we will give good reason.

  • Why is this only available on iOS?

    Resources are limited! We want to develop another mobile app for Android and also make a website, but we're short on time and cash to get the job done. Since Scout is currently free to all users (beyond donations), we don't make a profit! However, if you'd really like to see an Android or website created, feel free to reach out and help us contribute however you can!

  • When will posting be available outside of South Carolina?

    We're rolling the app out in waves, so once we work out the bugs and kinks, we'll quickly open it up to the southeastern states, then to the entire United States, then North America, and finally worldwide! We appreciate your patience during our trial period.
